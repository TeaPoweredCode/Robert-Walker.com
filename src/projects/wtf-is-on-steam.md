The '<a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLTFohR7GUZYcD8t4bbSKYpnsjMWf19Qgo" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WTF is... *Game*</a>' was a first impressions video game youtube series by John Bain aka (<a href="https://www.youtube.com/@cynicalbrit" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">TotalBiscuit</a>). 
At his height, TotalBiscuit was releasing a video every day getting 300-500k views.

The 'WTF is on Steam' browser extension was designed to help players make more informed purchasing decisions.
While browsing the <a href="https://store.steampowered.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Steam</a> store, it highlighted games that had an associated WTF Is… review and embedded the video directly on the game's Steam page.

Sadly, TotalBiscuit passed away in 2018 ending the series.
